Ian Ziering: See Photos of the 90210 &amp; Sharknado Actor

Publish date: 2024-06-03
Shutterstock

Ian Ziering is an actor best known for playing Steve Sanders on ‘Beverly Hills, 90210,’ which he played from 1990 to 2000. Playing Steve was his breakout role and made him a major star alongside castmates Tori Spelling, Luke Perry, Jason Priestley, Shannen Doherty, Brian Austin Green, Jennie Garth, and more. In addition to ‘90210,’ Ian notably starred as Fin Shepard in the ‘Sharknado’ film series with Tara Reid. Ian also starred as DC Comics character Blue Devil in the series ‘Swamp Thing,’ which is making its broadcast debut in October 2020 after previously airing on DC Universe. Ian appeared alongside many of his castmates for the ‘Beverly Hills, 90210’ revival in 2019, but the series was canceled after one season.
