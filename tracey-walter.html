Tracey Walter has played sidekicks and ‘henchmen’ in films and television shows such as ‘Batman’ as Bob the Goon, ‘City Slickers as Cookie, and ‘Conan the Destroyer’ as Malak. Walter has also acted in Jonathan Demme films such as “Something Wild” in 1986, “Married to the Mob” in 1988, “The Silence of the Lambs” in 1991, “Philadelphia” in 1993, “Beloved” in 1998, and “The Manchurian Candidate” in 2004.

In the film ‘Erin Brockovich,’ he played Charles Embry in 2000.
