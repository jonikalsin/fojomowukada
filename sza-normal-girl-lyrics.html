SZA Normal Girl Lyrics

Publish date: 2024-08-08

“Normal Girl” is a song by American singer-songwriter SZA. It was included in her debut studio album “Ctrl”, which was released in 2017. The song features SZA reflecting on her personal life and the pressures of fame, as she sings about wanting to be seen as a “normal girl”.

About

SZA, whose real name is Solána Imani Rowe, is an American singer-songwriter. She was born on November 8, 1989, in St. Louis, Missouri, and raised in Maplewood, New Jersey. SZA began her music career in 2012 with the release of her debut EP, “See.SZA.Run”.

She gained wider recognition with the release of her debut studio album, “Ctrl”, in 2017, which received critical acclaim and commercial success. The album explored themes of self-discovery, relationships, and empowerment, and featured collaborations with artists such as Travis Scott, James Fauntleroy, and Isaiah Rashad.

SZA’s music is known for its blend of R&B, neo-soul, and alternative hip-hop. Her vocals are often described as emotive and vulnerable, and her lyrics frequently touch on personal experiences and social issues. In addition to her music career, SZA has also ventured into acting and modelling.

SZA has been nominated for several awards, including Grammy Awards, BET Awards, Soul Train Music Awards, and Billboard Music Awards. She has won several awards, including the MTV Video Music Award for Best Visual Effects for her collaboration with Travis Scott on the song “Love Galore”.
