Meet All of Us Are Dead Actress Park Ji-hu! Her Bio, Boyfriend, Height

Publish date: 2024-04-27

Netflix’s South Korean horror series All Of Us Are Dead premiered on January 28, 2022, and it saw a group of students trapped on a campus premise amongst zombies. Our star, Park Ji-hu portrayed one of the survivors there named “On-jo,” who joined forces with other students to take on the mass of the brain-eating creatures.

So, who is she? Get to know her real-life details as this Park Ji-hu Bio proceeds.

Park Ji-hu On All of Us Are Dead

Park Ji-hu’s character “On-jo” is one of the students of Hyosan High School where a deadly virus outbreak turned almost all of the students into zombies. So, trapped without any food, cell phone service, or adults, she and other teenagers struggle to survive, all while hoping that someone is on the way to rescue them.

But thanks to the survival knowledge gained from her firefighter dad, On-jo can keep her composure. Moreover, besides saving herself, this fighter lady was also able to save a few of her friends out of there alive.

The plot is similar to that of the popular South Korean zombie horror film Train From Busan, and we’re not the only one who thinks so. As seen in the trailer, one of the characters in the series references the film as well.

This season, Park was joined by co-stars — Yoon Chan-young (Chung-san), Cho Yi-hyun (Nam-ra), Park Solomon (Lee Soo-hyuk), Yoo In-soo (Yoon Gwi-nam), and Shin Jae-hwi (Chang-hoon).

Most of them including Park is likely a new face to you. But you might have already seen Lee Yoo-Mi as Player 240 in Netflix’s hit survival drama Squid Game. Sadly, she only has a small role in All Of Us Are Dead.

According to the series co-director Lee Jae-kyoo, they intentionally cast lesser-known actors, in the hopes that it would help the show feel more realistic and shocking.

Trivia: Park attended a photoshoot alongside her co-stars for Cosmopolitan before the show’s premiere in Jan 2022.

Does Park Ji-hu Have A Boyfriend?

No, Park Ji-hu didn’t seem like she had a boyfriend as of 2022. Well, in case she did! We commend the actress for keeping her partner a secret.

Also, we did check all of her socials and found no traces of her love life.

It could be that the star was busy filming All Of Us Are Dead and was solely focused on building her career. 

A day before the Netflix series premiered, Park took it to her IG to share two posts captioned, “Only on Netflix #now” and “I’m Nam Onjo @netflixkr #Now our school” (translated from Korean).

Park Ji-hu Movies And TV Shows

Park Ji-hu’s on-screen credits are listed below:

Movies: Black Light (2020) as “Eun-yeong”, House of Hummingbird (2018) as “Eun-hee”, The Witness (2018) as “Ye-Seul”,  Fabricated City (2017) as “high-school student”, Vanishing Time: A Boy Who Returned (2016) as “Girl resembling Su-rin,” and Home Without Me (2016).

TV series: All of Us Are Dead (2022) as “Nam Ohn Jo/On-jo”, and Beautiful World (2019) as “Jung Da-hee”.

As of 2022, she was managed by BH entertainment. The same agency that managed Kim Go Eun, Park Bo Young, Park Hae Soo, Yoo Ji Tae, Lee Byung Hun, Lee Jin Wook, Han Hyo Joo, and so on

Also, she made her first vlog for the agency introducing herself in Jan 2022. Do check it out over BH Entertainment’s official Youtube channel.

This talented actress also has multiple accolades entitled to her name. Listing them are the 18th Tribeca Film Festival for Best Actress, 39th Korean Association of Film Critics Award, 8th Best Star Awards, 4th London East Asia Film Festival, 19th Director’s Cut Awards, Cine 21 Awards for Best New Actress, and 7th Wildflower Film Awards for Best Actress.
