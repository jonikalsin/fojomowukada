Are Dustin Rhodes and Cody Rhodes half-brothers?

Cody and Dustin Rhodes, also referred to as Cody Rhodes and Goldust, The Brotherhood, The Rhodes Brothers, and Gold and Stardust, were a Cody Rhodes and Dustin Rhodes-led tag team in American professional wrestling.

They are also well-known for their time in All Elite Wrestling and WWE (AEW). Real-life half-brothers Cody and Dustin Rhodes were formerly under the management of their father, the legendary wrestler Dusty Rhodes. They belonged to a section of The Elite.

The tag team was created after Cody was fired from the WWE for losing to Randy Orton in a bout that was sparked by Cody’s criticism of Triple H’s authority as the organization’s COO.
