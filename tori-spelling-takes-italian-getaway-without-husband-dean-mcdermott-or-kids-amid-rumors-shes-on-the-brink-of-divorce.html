Tori Spelling takes Italian getaway without husband Dean McDermott or kids amid rumors shes on th

Publish date: 2024-07-03

TORI Spelling is living it up on an Italian getaway without her husband, Dean McDermott, or their kids as rumors of a divorce have not disappeared.

The actress has continued to fuel the rumors with eye-opening posts on her social media.

Tori took to her Instagram Stories to capture her trip in Italy, as she is currently staying at the Borgo Santandrea in Amalfi.

In one Story, the star stands alongside two friends as they all hold drinks.

“The only thing missing from this is you Whitney Cummings,” Tori captioned it, referring to the standup comic, and her friend.

The 48-year-old then updated her fans of her current location as she said in a short clip: “… I feel honored to have found a rarity. I feel like people are always telling me about the best places in Europe and I’m always like, ‘Um, I don’t know it. I’m a mom. I work. I don’t do anything.’ And now here I am…”

Tori, who has lately been compared to Khloe Kardashian, then began to gush about the hotel, describing how it all looks, before saying, “Thank you. We love it here.”

The Beverly Hills, 90210 alum, who recently revealed she was leaving her children behind “to work on a new project,” is also currently working in Italy and was previously in London.

Amid all of the traveling and working, Tori’s husband, Dean, has been missing from all of her social media updates.

Tori shares five kids with Dean– however, the famous couple are speculated to be going through a rough patch in their marriage.

THE END OF TORI AND DEAN?

Previously, a source exclusively revealed to The Sun that Dean, 54, “wants to file for divorce” but “can’t afford it.”

The insider continued: “Tori and Dean are totally stuck together right now. Neither of them can afford to go through divorce proceedings.

“They have five kids and a ton of pets. Their home is always a disaster- it’s just a total mess.”

The source added at the time: “Right now divorce isn’t on the horizon because they can’t swing it financially. They are in a living nightmare.”

The actor was also spotted out in Los Angeles without his wedding ring, leading to more fan speculation.

WHERE’S DEAN?

It also didn’t help that Tori recently welcomed the fall season by showing off some personalized Halloween pumpkins with her and her children’s’ names carved into them, including one that read “The McDermott Family.”

Fans, however, thought the pumpkin preparations were a dead give away that Tori and Dean have broken up, because his name was not included in the personalized carvings.
