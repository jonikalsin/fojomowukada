Biography

Yeng Constantino was born in Rodriguez, Philippines on December 4, 1988. Initially famous as the Season 1 winner of Pinoy Dream Academy, she went on to release a multi-Platinum debut album titled Salamat. Her subsequent recordings include Journey, Metamorphosis, and All About Love. In 2016, she appeared as a judge on Pinoy Boyband Superstar. She is a cousin of Filipino reality star Robi Domingo. In 2006, she defeated competitor Jay-R Siaboc on the first season of Pinoy Dream Academy.
