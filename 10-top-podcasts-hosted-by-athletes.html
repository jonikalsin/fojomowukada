10 Top Podcasts Hosted by Athletes

Publish date: 2024-06-09

Podcasts have become a popular medium for athletes to connect with their fans, share personal stories, and discuss various topics including sports, lifestyle, and entertainment. Many athletes have ventured into the world of podcasting, creating engaging content that resonates with a wide audience. Here are 10 top podcasts hosted by athletes that offer unique insights, inspiring stories, and entertaining conversations.

1. The LeBron James Podcast

Host: LeBron James Description: The LeBron James Podcast provides listeners with an inside look into the life and career of one of the greatest basketball players of all time. LeBron discusses a wide range of topics, including his journey to NBA stardom, philanthropy, and social issues.

2. The Tom Brady Podcast

Host: Tom Brady Description: The Tom Brady Podcast offers an intimate glimpse into the life of the legendary NFL quarterback. From discussions about health and wellness to interviews with fellow athletes, Tom Brady shares valuable insights on and off the field.

3. Uninterrupted with Serena Williams

Host: Serena Williams Description: Uninterrupted with Serena Williams features the tennis icon in candid conversations with athletes, celebrities, and industry leaders. Serena's podcast covers topics such as women in sports, empowerment, and life as a professional athlete.

4. The Players' Tribune Podcast with Kevin Durant

Host: Kevin Durant Description: Hosted by NBA star Kevin Durant, The Players' Tribune Podcast features in-depth interviews with athletes from various sports. The podcast provides a platform for athletes to share their personal stories and perspectives.

5. The R2C2 Podcast with CC Sabathia and Ryan Ruocco

Hosts: CC Sabathia and Ryan Ruocco Description: The R2C2 Podcast, hosted by retired MLB pitcher CC Sabathia and sports broadcaster Ryan Ruocco, combines sports, pop culture, and entertainment. The duo engages in lively discussions with guests ranging from athletes to musicians.

6. The Selfmade Podcast with Nadeshot

Host: Nadeshot Description: The Selfmade Podcast, hosted by esports icon Nadeshot, delves into the world of gaming, entrepreneurship, and self-improvement. Nadeshot invites guests from the gaming and business industries for insightful conversations.

7. The Tidal League Podcast with Chris Bosh and Dwyane Wade

Hosts: Chris Bosh and Dwyane Wade Description: The Tidal League Podcast, hosted by former NBA stars Chris Bosh and Dwyane Wade, covers basketball, culture, and lifestyle. The hosts engage with guests to explore a wide array of topics beyond the basketball court.

8. The How Hungry Are You? Podcast with Serge Ibaka

Host: Serge Ibaka Description: The How Hungry Are You? Podcast, hosted by NBA player Serge Ibaka, features entertaining and unscripted conversations with fellow athletes, celebrities, and chefs. Serge's charismatic hosting style makes for engaging and humorous episodes.

9. The EARN Your Leisure Podcast with Andrew Hawkins

Host: Andrew Hawkins Description: The EARN Your Leisure Podcast, hosted by former NFL wide receiver Andrew Hawkins, focuses on financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and wealth-building. The podcast features interviews with successful entrepreneurs and financial experts.

10. The Suiting Up Podcast with Paul Rabil

Host: Paul Rabil Description: The Suiting Up Podcast, hosted by professional lacrosse player Paul Rabil, explores the intersection of sports, business, and performance. Paul interviews athletes, coaches, and entrepreneurs to uncover insights into success and high performance.

These podcasts hosted by athletes offer a diverse range of content, providing listeners with valuable perspectives, inspiring stories, and entertainment. Whether you're a sports enthusiast, an aspiring entrepreneur, or someone interested in personal development, these podcasts have something for everyone. Tune in to gain unique insights from the world of sports and beyond.
