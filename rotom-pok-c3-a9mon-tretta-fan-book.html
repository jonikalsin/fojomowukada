Rotom (Japanese: ロトム Rotom) is a Normal-class Rookie Tretta. It is one of the Promotional Tretta.

Tretta data

Pokémon description
Oh! Rotom has snuck inside the Tretta machine!

Release information

This Tretta was available as an insert with the Winter 2014 issue of Pokémon Tretta Fan Book, released on December 15, 2014.

Trivia

Origin

Astonish is a move that Rotom can learn in the games. The Rotom depicted is based on the Rotom distributed from certain Pokémon Tretta machines starting mid-October 2014.
