Fernando del Solar, a television presenter, model, and actor, died on Thursday, June 1, 2022.

The National Association of Interpreters of Mexico and Azteca Noticias both verified the presenter’s death.

Fernando Del Solar cause of death

Although the official circumstances of his death are still unknown, Del Solar had been battling Hodgkin’s lymphoma for years, a malignancy in the lymph nodes that damages the lymphatic system that he was diagnosed with in 2012, according to what he claimed in a 2020 interview with TVAzteca.

Fernando Del Solar wife and children

Del Solar began dating television presenter Ingrid Coronado in 2008, with whom he married on May 5, 2012 and has two children, Luciano and Paolo. They divorced in 2015. It was stated that the reason for their divorce was due to troubles they both had, Coronado’s negative connection with Fernando’s family, and also cancer he was suffering from.

Del Solar married Anna Ferro for the second time on March 22, 2022, and remained with her until the end of his life.
