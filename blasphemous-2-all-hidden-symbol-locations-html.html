Intro

There are 10 hidden symbols for players to find in Blasphemous 2. These hidden symbols are located throughout Cvstodia and are either very obvious to spot or well hidden.

This guide will help you locate them all. Collecting all 10 of these symbols unlocks the Still Among Us achievement and some additional rewards.

Get the Chime of the Twisted One Prayer

Before you can start collecting the symbols you need to first acquire a specific Prayer. This Prayer is called the Chime of the Twisted One and it is located in the room to the right of the teleport room in Mother of Mothers. To collect a symbol use this Prayer when you are near it.

In the same room as the Prayer you will notice 10 symbols on the wall behind. These symbols track your progress in collecting. Each time you collect a symbol it will light up on this wall and a reward will appear in this room you can return to collect.

Easter Egg Room

If you slide to the right of the chest where you get the Prayer you can enter what appears to be an Easter Egg room. This room has two statues in it with the writings Erlopeh and Serena on them.

Hidden Symbol 1: Repose of the Silent One

There is a hidden symbol on the casket the Penitent One wakes up in at the start of the game. It is located in the first room of the Repose of the Silent One.

Hidden Symbol 2: Mother of Mothers

In the second Prie Dieu room in Mother of Mothers go to the right to encounter a large dead tree from the first game. Use your Prayer when standing by the tree to have the hidden symbol appear on it.

Hidden Symbol 3: Sea of Ink

In the last room of the Sea of Ink just before you enter the Sunken Cathedral you will see rows of statues above you. The second statue from the entrance to the cathedral has the hidden symbol in his hand.

Hidden Symbol 4: City of the Blessed Name

Once you’ve gotten the Scion’s Protection ability you can climb up to a bell in the middle of the City of the Blessed Name (between the portal room and the Cobijada room). On this bell you will find a symbol.

Hidden Symbol 5: Two Moons

In the Two Moons location go to the reflection side of the area (by using the fountains) and make your way to the longest vertical room. In this room go to the top right corner. In this corner you will find a mirror. Break the mirror to reveal the symbol.

Hidden Symbol 6: Mother of Mothers

In the second Prie Dieu room in Mother of Mothers go to the right to encounter a large dead tree from the first game. Use your Prayer when standing by the tree to have the hidden symbol appear on it.

Hidden Symbol 7: Sea of Ink

In the last room of the Sea of Ink just before you enter the Sunken Cathedral you will see rows of statues above you. The second statue from the entrance to the cathedral has the hidden symbol in his hand.

Hidden Symbol 8: City of the Blessed Name

Once you’ve gotten the Scion’s Protection ability you can climb up to a bell in the middle of the City of the Blessed Name (between the portal room and the Cobijada room). On this bell you will find a symbol.

Hidden Symbol 9: Two Moons

In the Two Moons location go to the reflection side of the area (by using the fountains) and make your way to the longest vertical room. In this room go to the top right corner. In this corner you will find a mirror. Break the mirror to reveal the symbol.

Hidden Symbol 10: Choir of Thorns

Go to the furthest left Prie Dieu in the Choir of Thorns. From there head right two rooms so you reach the square shown on the map above (you will be standing by some spikes). Use the Prayer here and the hidden symbol will appear on the wall in the bramble. Found by DVD copy of Shark Tale (with assist from Nameless).
