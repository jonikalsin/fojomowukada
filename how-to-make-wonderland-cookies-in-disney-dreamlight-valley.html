How To Make Wonderland Cookies In Disney Dreamlight Valley

Publish date: 2024-07-04

The Wonderland Cookies is a four-star Dessert category food item in Disney Dreamlight Valley inspired by Disney’s Alice in Wonderland franchise, making it an appealing meal that players want to make for more than one reason. But since its recipe is hidden, players want to know how to make Wonderland Cookies in Disney Dreamlight Valley. To avoid spending unnecessary time experimenting and wasting ingredients. Continue reading our guide as we share the recipe for the said meal and walk you through the process of how to make Wonderland Cookies in Disney Dreamlight Valley. 

How To Make Wonderland Cookies In Disney Dreamlight Valley

Since Wonderland Cookies is a four-star meal in Disney Dreamlight Valley, you need four ingredients to make it. 

  • 1x Wheat
  • 1x Butter
  • 1x Sugarcane
  • 1x Vanilla

You can get Wheat and its seeds from Goofy’s Stall in the Peaceful Meadows biome. If you want to make Wonderland Cookies in abundance, you can save Star Coins by farming Wheat instead of purchasing it directly from Goofy. Next, we have Butter, a dairy item you can buy from Chez Remy’s Pantry using Star Coins. 

Sugarcane is yet another item you can obtain from Goofy’s Stall, but this time, from the Dazzle Beach biome. You can purchase Sugarcane or its seed, and the choice is yours. As for the last ingredient, Vanilla, you can forage it from plants found in the Sunlit Plateau biome. 

Once you have gathered the required ingredients, you can interact with a Cooking Station, add the ingredients into the Cooking Pot and initiate the cooking process by clicking on the “Start Cooking” button, which consumes one piece of Coal. 

After the cooking process ends, you will have the Wonderland Cookies on display, which will soon move to your inventory. On the same note, make sure you have an empty slot in your inventory, or else you won’t be able to make the Wonderland Cookies in Disney Dreamlight Valley. 

That concludes our guide on how to make Wonderland Cookies in Disney Dreamlight Valley.
